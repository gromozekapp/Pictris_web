How to play

Classic Tetris controls with swipe gestures. Your gallery is the backdrop.

← →

Swipe left / right

Move the falling block and browse your gallery.

↑

Swipe up

Rotate the block.

↓

Swipe down

Hard-drop the block and switch to the next photo or video.

●

Long press

Pause the game — and video playback in Video Gallery mode — to take a closer look.

In-game tools

  • Eye (left) — preview the next photo or video in your queue
  • Period filter — browse by day, week, month, year, or all
  • Trash — delete the current item from your library
  • Score panel — high scores, photos viewed, and achievements

Three play modes

Choose how you want to rediscover your memories.

Photo Gallery

Your photos become the game background. Swipe down to hard-drop a block and switch to the next image.

Video Gallery

Portrait videos play behind the board with sound. Long press pauses the game and video together.
