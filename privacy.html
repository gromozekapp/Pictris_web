Privacy Policy for PicTris

Last Updated: July 5, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to PicTris. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data when you use our application.

Summary

PicTris is designed with privacy at its core:

  • Core gameplay works completely offline
  • No data collection
  • No cloud services
  • No user accounts
  • No analytics
  • No advertisements

Data Collection and Usage

What We Don't Collect

  • Personal information
  • Usage statistics
  • Location data
  • Device information
  • Photos or media files
  • Network information (except when you purchase PicTris Pro)

Photo Access

  • PicTris requires access to your photo library to function
  • All photo processing happens locally on your device
  • No photos are uploaded, stored, or transmitted
  • Photo access is used solely for gameplay and meme creation
  • You can revoke photo access at any time through your device settings

Camera and Microphone (Blogger Mode)

  • Blogger Mode uses the front camera as a live game background
  • The microphone records voice commentary during gameplay recordings
  • Camera and microphone access are optional and only used in Blogger Mode
  • Recordings are processed and stored locally on your device
  • You can grant or revoke camera and microphone access in iOS Settings

Saving Recordings

  • Blogger Mode can save gameplay recordings to your Photos library
  • Saving requires add-only photo library permission
  • Recordings are not uploaded or shared unless you choose to share them

In-App Purchases (PicTris Pro)

  • PicTris Pro (monthly subscription or lifetime purchase) is optional
  • Purchases are processed entirely by Apple through StoreKit
  • We do not collect or store payment information
  • An internet connection is required only to complete or restore a purchase
  • The core game (Photo Gallery and Video Gallery) remains free and works offline

Data Storage

Local Storage

  • All app data is stored locally on your device
  • Game progress and settings are saved only on your device
  • Created memes and Blogger Mode recordings are saved to your local photo library
  • No cloud backup or sync features

Data Deletion

  • Uninstalling the app removes all app-related data
  • Created memes and saved recordings remain in your photo library until you delete them
  • You can delete photos through the app's interface during gameplay

Technical Implementation

Offline Functionality

  • Photo Gallery and Video Gallery work entirely offline
  • Blogger Mode recording and playback work offline
  • No background data collection
  • No remote servers for gameplay or media

Security

  • No transmission of your photos, videos, or recordings
  • Standard iOS security measures apply
  • Purchase transactions are handled by Apple's secure payment system

Third-Party Services

PicTris does not:

  • Use third-party analytics
  • Integrate social media services
  • Include advertising networks
  • Use tracking technologies
  • Share data with third parties

PicTris Pro uses Apple In-App Purchase (StoreKit) only. Apple's privacy policy applies to payment processing.

Children's Privacy

  • PicTris is suitable for all ages
  • We do not collect any personal information
  • Parents can control photo library, camera, and microphone access through device settings

Your Rights

You have complete control over photo library access, camera and microphone access (Blogger Mode), local data deletion, app removal, and PicTris Pro subscription management via Apple ID settings.

Contact Information

  • Developer: Peresvet

Compliance

PicTris complies with App Store Guidelines, GDPR, CCPA, and COPPA.

Conclusion

PicTris is designed to provide a private, secure experience where your data stays on your device. We believe in transparent privacy practices and minimal data collection.

By using PicTris, you agree to this privacy policy. If you disagree with any part of this policy, please do not use the application.
